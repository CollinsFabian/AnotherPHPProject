PHP backend. SPA frontend. One clean boundary.

Keep the backend simple and let the frontend breathe.

Ziro is for people who want their PHP app to stay focused on real backend work: auth, business logic, data, and APIs. The frontend runs as a proper SPA, so the product feels modern without turning the backend into a pile of page glue.

Why Ziro

Built for teams that want their stack to make sense six months later.

Backend stays backend

Your PHP code handles auth, data, policies, and business logic instead of trying to also be a page rendering layer.

Future mobile support is easier

When the API is already the contract, adding a mobile client later feels like an extension, not a rewrite.

Small enough to understand

No giant maze of conventions. Just routes, middleware, services, templates, and a frontend entry you can trace quickly.

Static frontend delivery

The SPA builds into public, serves fast, and keeps runtime pressure off PHP where it matters.

Practical developer flow

You get template fragments, one app stylesheet, and a local router that behaves like production.

Less cleanup later

Starting with a cleaner boundary now means fewer painful migrations when the product gets bigger.
